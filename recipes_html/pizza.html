Pizza

Description

Pizza is quite a tasty and simple food. That is why it is many people's favorite. You can keep it plain with no toppings or you can add a wide variety of toppings such pepperoni, tomatoes, extra cheese, mushrooms, onions, pineapple, and ham.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tamato Sauce
  • Mozzeralla(or other cheese)
  • Various toppings(Optional)

Recipe

  1. Spread out the pizza dough on a plate
  2. Spread out a layer of tomato sauce over the layer of dough
  3. Sprinkle the cheese over the sauce
  4. If you have toppings now is the time to put them on the pizza
  5. Put the pizza in your oven for about 15 minutes
